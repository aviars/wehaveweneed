    Welcome to We Have We Need

    The aftermath of the Haiti Earthquake represents a valiant relief effort on the ground. And we, a group of compassionate geeks, gathered at CrisisCamps to use technology as a way ease this tremendous burden. The WeHaveWeNeed project is our humble attempt to better align the most urgent needs reported by those on the ground with the most generous donations.

    WeHaveWeNeed is a place where relief organizations can quickly post their most urgent needs and have them matched by generous donors during a time of crisis. This site was built by a group of geeky do-gooders who saw this as an opportunity to use technology to help bring people and donations together in the aftermath of a devastating earthquake in Haiti. If you'd like to participate in the program, please let us know.
